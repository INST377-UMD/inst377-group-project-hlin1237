Purpose/Goal

Our primary goal is to address the escalating crime rates in PG County, specifically focusing on incidents occurring from July 2023 to the present day. We aim to develop a robust system that not only provides real-time alerts to users but also offers valuable insights into the most prevalent crimes across different districts of the county. By doing so, our purpose is to empower residents and visitors, enhancing their safety and overall sense of security.

Mission Statement

At SecureWatch PG, our mission is to leverage technology to create a safer environment for the residents and visitors of PG County. We are committed to delivering real-time information on crime incidents, allowing users to stay informed and make educated decisions about their safety. Through our innovative application, we strive to contribute to the reduction of crime rates and foster a community that is both informed and proactive in addressing security concerns.
