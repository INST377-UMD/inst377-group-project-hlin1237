FAQ

Q:What is the primary goal of SecureWatch PG?
A: The primary goal is to address escalating crime rates in PG County, focusing on incidents from July 2023 to the present. The aim is to provide real-time alerts and insights into prevalent crimes, empowering residents and visitors for enhanced safety.
Q:What is SecureWatch PG's mission?
A: The mission is to leverage technology for a safer environment in PG County. SecureWatch PG is committed to delivering real-time information on crime incidents, allowing users to stay informed, make educated decisions, and contribute to the reduction of crime rates in a proactive community.
Q:What can users expect on the home page?
A: Users can expect an interactive experience with dynamic incident markers that refresh on each visit. Clicking on a marker reveals real-time details like date, location, and incident type. The Prince George County map, highlighted in red, offers an exclusive feature, providing a breakdown of each incident and their monthly occurrences. Additionally, the home page showcases the ten most recent incidents.
Q: What does the dedicated help page offer?
A: The help page serves as a user-friendly platform for submitting feedback and finding contact information. It provides visitors with a responsive avenue for interaction and support.
